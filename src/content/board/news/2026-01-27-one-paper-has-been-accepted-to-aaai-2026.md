A paper titled “OceanSplat: Object-aware Gaussian Splatting with Trinocular View Consistency for Underwater Scene Reconstruction” has been accepted to Annual AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AAAI) 2026.

The paper will be presented at AAAI 2026, to be held on January 20–27, 2026, in Singapore.
